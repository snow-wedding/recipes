Chinese Lamb Soup

Description

This very simple recipe produces a delicious lamb soup that can use either lamb chunks or slices. The vinegar reduces the gamey taste of the lamb, and the clear broth is easy to consume even on a weaker stomach.

Ingredients

  • Lamb (sliced or cubed)
  • Cilantro
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Add water to pot and set to boil.
  2. Add lamb to boiling water and salt to preference.
  3. Add vinegar and pepper to taste.
  4. Once water is boiling again and meat is cooked, turn off stove.
  5. Add cilantro and mix.
