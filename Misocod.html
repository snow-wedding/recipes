Nobu Style Miso Glazed Black Cod

Description

This is an recipe adapted from the famous miso black cod served at Nobu's restaurant. It's fairly simple to make, though it requires some time to marinate.

Ingredients

  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Miso paste
  • Black cod fillets

Steps

  1. Boil sake and mirin together at high heat to boil and evaporate alcohol.
  2. Turn heat to low, add miso paste, and mix.
  3. After miso has dissolved, turn heat to high and add sugar.
  4. After sugar has dissolved, remove from heat and cool to room temperature.
  5. Dry black cod fillets and coat with sauce from previous steps.
  6. Wrap with plastic and refrigerate for a few days.
  7. Once ready to cook, preheat oven to 400F and place foil over baking pan.
  8. Bake fish in oven for about 10 minutes with skin side up until surface browns.
  9. Flip and broil for about 5 minutes.
  10. Remove and serve once fish is opaque and flaky.
