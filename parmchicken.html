Parmesan Chicken and Basalmic Noodles

Description

This recipe is a combination of basalmic tomato sauce and paremsan chicken derived from Hello Fresh deliveries.

Ingredients for basalmic sauce

  • chives
  • parsley
  • oregano
  • tomatoes
  • garlic
  • basil
  • basalmic vinegar
  • oil
  • salt and pepper

Ingredients for parmesan chicken

  • panko
  • parmesan
  • paprika
  • olive oil
  • sour cream
  • salt and pepper
  • chicken cutlets
  • lemon

Steps

For basalmic tomato sauce

  1. Heat oil in pan
  2. Add tomatoes and Italian seasoning/oregano
  3. Add some basalmic vinegar and small amount of sugar
  4. Let it thicken a bit
  5. Stir in some butter till it melts
  6. Add the rest of the stuff (chives, parsley, basil
  7. Add salt and pepper

For parmesan chicken

  1. In a bowl, mix panko, paprika, parmesan, olive oil, salt, and pepper
  2. Spread top of chiken with sour cream
  3. Cover chicken with panko mix
  4. Roast (around 325F) in oven for 15-20 minutes
  5. Add some lemon juice before serving

Noodles

  1. Cook some noodles in pot
  2. Strain and add butter, salt, pepper, scallions, and garlic
  3. Mix basalmic sauce into noodles and serve with chicken
